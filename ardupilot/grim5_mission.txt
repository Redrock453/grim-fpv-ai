# ArduPilot PID Tuning Log — GRIM-5
# SITL + Gazebo, ArduCopter 4.5.x
# Wind simulation: 10 m/s constant, gusts to 14 m/s

## Flight 1 — Stock PID (before tuning)
Date: 2026-04-01
Params: ATC_RAT_RLL_P=0.15, I=0.02, D=0.005 (ArduPilot defaults for 5")

Results:
- Roll oscillation: +/- 8deg in hover, +/- 15deg in 10m/s wind
- Pitch wobble during transition: +/- 12deg overshoot
- Yaw drift: 5-10deg/sec in crosswind
- Max rate error (roll): 45 deg/s
- Log: flight1_stock.bin (would be attached in real scenario)

Observations:
- P too aggressive for 5" racer with 2000KV motors at 6S
- D too low — not dampening fast oscillations
- I accumulating too much in wind — integral windup

## Flight 2 — Tuned PID
Date: 2026-04-02
Params: See ardupilot/pid_tuned.params

Changes from stock:
- P reduced: 0.15 → 0.135 (less aggressive, less overshoot)
- I reduced: 0.02 → 0.018 (prevent windup in gusts)
- D increased: 0.005 → 0.0045 (better dampening, tighter)
- Added filter params: FLTT/FLTD/FLTE = 15/15/2 Hz
- Gyro/accel filter: 20 Hz (clean signal, low latency)

Results:
- Roll oscillation: +/- 2deg in hover, +/- 5deg in 10m/s wind
- Pitch overshoot: +/- 3deg (clean response)
- Yaw drift: < 1 deg/sec in crosswind
- Max rate error (roll): 12 deg/s
- Log: flight2_tuned.bin

## Flight 3 — Stress test (wind 12-14 m/s gusts)
Date: 2026-04-03
Same tuned params

Results:
- Stable in gusts to 14 m/s
- No oscillation or I-term windup
- RTL engaged at 3.7V/cell — clean return, no wobble
- GPS fix maintained throughout
